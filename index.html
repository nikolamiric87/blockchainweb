About Blockchain

  • - Blockchain technology is a digital ledger that records value creation in a Transparent & Non-manipulated;
  • - Democratic & Decentralised and Safe & Trastfull way.
  • - With keeping a definite record of transactions which provides trust & traceability the areas of value creation are in Financial sector – Cryptocurencies;
  • - Supply chain and Energy & Different utilies sectors;
  • - Governance & Public services and in designing a new business models.

Our 7 Governance Principles of EUBCHUB Serbia

- Concern for Community (realization of Sustainable Development Goals - SDGs)
- Democratic Member Control
- Member Economic Participation
- Autonomy and Independence
- Education, Skill Acquisition and Access to Information
- Cooperation among Cooperatives
- Voluntary and Open Membership

Services

Advocacy

EUBCHUB Serbia facilitates advocacy for creation and development of Blockchain and emerging technologies. We aim to support the Sustainable Development Goals across multiple sectors worldwide.

Capacity Building

EUBCHUB Serbia generates and distributes knowledge about Blockchain and emerging technologies. We create new concepts throughout open research & innovation. We design curriculums and organise events with the aim to generate & share knowledge.

Projects

EUBCHUB Serbia initiates, co-ordinates and runs Blockchain and emerging technologies projects of common interest of the co-operative stakeholders, members and partners.
